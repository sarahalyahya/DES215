Parallax Scrolling

Notice how the background stays in place as you scroll

How It Works

The parallax sections have background-attachment: fixed; which makes the background image stay in place while you scroll.

This regular content section scrolls normally over the background, creating a sense of depth and movement.

Keep scrolling to see more parallax sections and really notice the effect!

Different Background

Each section can have a different image

The Effect Gets Better

The more you scroll, the more obvious the parallax effect becomes. The background images appear to move at a different speed than the content.

This creates visual depth and makes your website feel more dynamic and engaging.

Professional sites use this technique to add polish and sophistication.

One CSS Property

background-attachment: fixed;

That's Parallax Scrolling!

Scroll back up to the top and watch the backgrounds again. Notice how they stay fixed while the white content sections scroll over them.

This simple technique makes your site feel premium and professional.

Try it on your site! Add background-attachment: fixed; to any section with a background image.
